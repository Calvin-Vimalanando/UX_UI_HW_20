For The Animals

A case study on a redesign for a NPO that stands as a wildlife conservation and rescue



Research

The initial research was done through a stakeholder interview conducted through a phone call with the founder of the NPO. This interview was done to see understand the direction that the founder was bringing forward with the organisation and what they feel is important and what information they would want to be displayed first.

I conducted 5 user interviews on the current website that they currently have and had them explore the website as well as go through the current donation system to discover problems with the current design.

Problem Statement

For the Animals is a wildlife charity that is designed with intent to raise funds and awareness towards rescuing and rehabilitating wildlife, protecting habitats and creating sustainable jobs. We have observed that the current site is outdated and lacks engagement. In the context of a non-for-profit, this can deter users from carrying out the donation process, as the current interface fails to instil feelings of trust and professionalism.
